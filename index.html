FIT FUSION

Fit Fusion – AI Powered Virtual Try-On E-Commerce Platform Fit Fusion is a real-time AI-powered e-commerce web application that allows users to virtually try on clothes before purchasing. The platform uses Artificial Intelligence to simulate how selected outfits would look on the user, providing a personalized and interactive shopping experience. Users can browse different categories of dresses, select their preferred outfit, and use the virtual try-on feature to preview the dress on a digital model or uploaded image. This helps customers make confident buying decisions and reduces product returns.
